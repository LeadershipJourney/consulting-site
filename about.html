About Yelena

Yelena Kutikova is an experiential learning and leadership development expert with a passion for designing transformative organizational and team experiences that unlock growth, connection, and purpose. She brings more than two decades of leadership development and talent programming experience across Fortune 500 companies, nonprofits, and global industries — from media and beauty to finance, technology, nonprofit, and consulting.

Her signature approach blends creativity with structure, AI innovation with human interaction, and inclusion with impact. Whether she's leading a strategic retreat, facilitating DISC or Gallup strengths‑based team sessions, or designing immersive leadership programs, Yelena creates spaces where employees connect and shine. What lights her up most is crafting intentional, energizing leadership development experiences that go beyond surface‑level activities.

Shaped by an international background and fluent in multiple languages, Yelena designs leadership experiences with cultural intelligence and global reach. She is certified in Gallup Strengths, DISC, Korn Ferry 360 Feedback, and continually explores how emerging technologies like artificial intelligence can enhance learning and leadership.
