Offerings

Customized Leadership Development Program

A scalable program tailored for professionals at every career stage.

Designed to meet leaders where they are, this program scales from emerging professionals to seasoned executives. Early Career: explore career navigation and build foundational skills.
Mid‑Level: strengthen core management competencies, feedback and team leadership.
Senior Executives: hone strategic leadership, executive presence and culture‑shaping capabilities.

Programs can be delivered in‑person, hybrid or virtual over several months or condensed into a 1–2 day immersive experience. Each engagement includes a pre‑program needs assessment, curated learning experiences, optional assessments (DISC, Gallup Strengths, 360) and follow‑up support.

Team Building Experience

A customized session designed to deepen team trust, communication and collaboration.

Tailored for your team’s unique dynamics, this experiential workshop deepens trust, communication and collaboration. Sessions can be hosted on‑site or offsite and include a pre‑session consultation, all materials and a post‑session recap. Available as half‑day or full‑day experiences.

DISC‑Based Team Workshop

Explore communication styles and team dynamics using the DISC framework.

Dive deeper into team communication styles and dynamics with the DISC framework. This interactive session includes individual DISC assessments, team profiles and facilitation. Offered as a half‑day experience.

1:1 Coaching (DISC or Gallup Strengths)

Personalized coaching to enhance self‑awareness, communication and leadership effectiveness.

Deepen your understanding of yourself and others through individualized coaching. Sessions focus on increasing self‑awareness, improving interpersonal effectiveness and leveraging individual strengths. Includes a choice of DISC or StrengthsFinder assessment, optional 360 input and a personalized development plan. Available as one‑time or multi‑session packages.

Chameleon Leadership™ Full‑Day Experience

A highly interactive day focused on adaptive leadership, emotional intelligence and authentic connection.

Designed for people managers, this immersive program develops adaptive leadership and emotional intelligence while driving performance through authentic connection. It can be a powerful standalone or part of a longer cohort‑based journey. Includes Chameleon Leadership assessments, a pre‑session alignment call and optional follow‑up.
