Offerings

Team Building Experience

A customized session designed to deepen team trust, communication, and collaboration.

Tailored for your team’s unique dynamics, this experiential workshop deepens trust, communication and collaboration. Sessions can be hosted on‑site or offsite and include a pre‑session consultation, all materials, and a post‑session recap. Recommended duration: half‑day (approximately three hours). Suitable for up to 20 participants.

DISC‑Based Team Workshop

Interactive team experience using DISC to explore communication styles and team dynamics.

Dive deeper into team communication styles and dynamics with the DISC framework. This interactive session includes individual DISC assessments, team profiles, customized insights and take‑home tools. Recommended as a half‑day experience for teams of up to 20 participants.

1:1 Coaching (DISC or Strengths)

Personalized coaching focused on self‑awareness, interpersonal effectiveness, and leveraging individual strengths.

Deepen your understanding of yourself and others through individualized coaching. Sessions focus on increasing self‑awareness, improving interpersonal effectiveness and leveraging personal strengths. Includes a DISC or StrengthsFinder assessment, optional 360 insights, and a personalized development plan.

Chameleon Leadership™ Full‑Day Experience

A highly interactive day focused on adaptive leadership, emotional intelligence, and authentic connection.

Designed for people managers, this immersive program develops adaptive leadership and emotional intelligence while driving performance through authentic connection. The full‑day experience includes the Chameleon Leadership assessment, a pre‑session alignment call and an optional follow‑up session.
